


\chapter*{Introduction}

This work explores the untyped and typed lambda calculus, intuitionistic logic, and the Curry-Howard Isomorphism. Selected theoretical concepts are further exemplified through implementations in Haskell and Lean4, leveraging Haskell's lazy evaluation for the untyped lambda calculus.
